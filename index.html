See how your music taste connects you to others. This visualization creates connections between listeners based on favourite artists, genres and demographics.

How it works

Complete the Google Form using the "Add an Entry" button.

If you are one of the first to submit, please check back later to see more results and discover your potential connections!

If you have any questions or feedback, please feel free to contact me at chenank@sheridancollege.ca.

What does it mean?

Each circle represents a person.

Refer to the Colour Guide and Symbol Guide for their meanings.

Lines connect users who share any Top Artist (#1, #2, or #3), and a pair can have multiple lines when they match on multiple artists (hover to see the exact match).

Click on a circle to view more details about that user.
